Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer by the websites that you visit and stored in your web browser while the user is browsing websites. Every time the user loads the website, the browser sends the cookie back to the server to notify the website of the user's previous activity. Cookies were designed to be a reliable mechanism for websites to remember stateful information or to record the user's browsing activity. They are widely used in order to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide business and marketing information to the owners of the site.
